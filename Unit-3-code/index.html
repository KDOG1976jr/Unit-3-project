Bowl for your life

You are in the Championship match for the state title for highschool bowling. Down 232-223 you need a strike to win the match and take home the trophy. After a bad shot in the last frame you need to make an adjustment to win. What do you do?

You release the ball down the lane. It makes a clean break towards the pins and strikes. You have won the State Championship.

The ball doesn't hook up enough and you leave a pocket 7-10, losing the match by one.

The ball miracously hits the pocket, but leaves a ten pin to tie the match. You go to a roll off to determine the winner and win by one pin.

The ball hits the pocket light leaving a 7 pin to tie the match. You go to a roll off to determine the winner and ultimately loose by one.

While celebrating your win with family and friends, you are approached by scouts from two of the top colleges in the country: Witcha State, and Indiana Tech. They both give you offers right there on the spot. You would go on to have a great college career, and would also go on to break the titles, and majors records in the PBA, establishing your spot in the PBA hall of Fame, retiring with millions of dollars in earnings and sponserships.

Despite not winning you are aproached by a scout from Indiana Weslyan University who gives you an offer to bowl. You go and become a star in college, but can't seem to translate that sucess into the PBA. Eventually you go from bowling professionally to being a coach, training the younger generation to perfect their craft.

While celebrating with family and friends, a scout for Purdue come up to you and gives you an offer to bowl for them. You accept and quickly take the world by storm, becoming known by almost evryone in the world gaining a huge following. In the PBA, you set records that haven't been touched in years. You retire with a place in the HOF, a huge reputation, millions in earnings, a happy marriage, and three kids, two of which you are following in your footsteps and becoming the next stars in the PBA.

You are offered by a couple smaller schools to bowl for them, but ultimately gain no recognition,=. You realize that making it to the pro's may be impossible and fall on your backup career of Website Development. You start a company and become one of the biggest Web Dev companies in the world, becoming the richest person in the world. You still bowl leagues, but in the bowling world, you will forever be known as that one person who always came up short.
